  • Brick-Force Aurora

    January 02, 2025

    Greetings Community,

     

    We're excited to announce that a group of passionate developers has taken up the mantle to revive Brick-Force! They have created a server emulator released under the name Brick-Force Aurora. This project aims to breathe new life into the game we all love and miss.

     

    The project is currently live, and the latest release, v1.3.1, brings the following features:

    • 🛒 Shop
    • 💀 Team Deathmatch
    • 🪖 Deathmatch
    • 🛠️ Build and Destroy
    • 🚩 Capture the Flag
    • 🔨 Build Mode
    • 🎒 Inventory System

     

    Join the Brick-Force Aurora community to learn more, contribute, or simply relive the nostalgia. Here are some helpful links to get started:

    • Discord - Join our community and discuss with other players and developers.
    • GitHub - Explore the project, contribute, or report issues.

     

    We want to thank everyone for their continued support and enthusiasm. Your loyalty and love for Brick-Force have made this possible. Let’s build, share, and play together again!

     

    See you in the game!

    Cheers!

    The Brick-Force Aurora Team
